What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?

Float lets different elements of a site share horizontal space. You might need to clear a float in order to only want one or a few elements to share horizontal space.

What do absolute, fixed, and relative do?

Fixed position keeps the fixed img on one location of the screen at all times. Relative keeps the img moved close to the normal position of the image while absolute is the reverse.
