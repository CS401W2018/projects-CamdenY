What is Marvel Rivals?

Marvel Rivals is the hit 2024 multiplayer third-person shooter featuring Marvel Comics heroes and villains as playable characters. Players assemble in six versus six matches and clash against each other in iconic locations in Comics, such as New York, Asgard and Wakanda. As a free-to-play game, Marvel Rivals has millions of active players.

The heroes in Marvel Rivals fit into three different categories: Vanguards, Duelists and Strategists.

Vanguards:
Vanguards are the tankiest class in the game. They are usually larger characters with lots of health and ways to protect from damage for other heroes. Heroes in this category include the Hulk, Doctor Strange, Captain America and Thor.

Duelists:
Duelists are the main damage dealers in the game. They have a variety of playstyles, but their main goal is to eliminate other players by doing as much damage as possible. Some Duelists in this game are Spider-Man, Wolverine, Iron Man and Scarlet Witch,

Strategists:
Strategists are the healers of the game. Strategists are responsible in keeping their teammates alive. Some of the heroes in this category include Loki, Invisible Woman, Mantis and Rocket Raccoon.
