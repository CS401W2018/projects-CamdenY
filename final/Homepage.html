Aloha Mai Kākou, Welcome!

This site is a summary of events that lead up to eventual overthrow of the Hawaiian Kingdom. Below you will find a brief summary of each of this website's pages.

Western Foreign Relations

This section will be about the important prior interactions to western foreigners and how they shaped the government. Some of these important interactions are the arrival of Captain Cook, Kamehameha's use of guns and cannons in his war to conquer all the islands, the arrival of missionaries and the Paulet affair.

King Kalākaua

This page will go over the reign of Kalākaua and how his and spending angered a lot of rich westerners.

The Bayonet Constitution

This page will summarize the formation of the Reform Party and how they forced Kalākaua to sign a new constitution to favor white foreigners.

Queen Liliʻuokalani

This page will summarize the years Liliʻuokalani ruled after her brother's death, and how she attempted to restore the previous constitution in spite of resistance.

The Overthrow

This section will go into great detail to show the timeline of the U.S backed coup d'etat of the Hawaiian Kingdom.
