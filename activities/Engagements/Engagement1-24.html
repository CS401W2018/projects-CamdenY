Engagement - Structural Tags

What goes in the header?

The name of your site or page goes in the header

What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?

The Nav leads to other parts of your website. It helps the viewer go to the part of the page they want to. The Nav doesn't have to be a part of the header, but it can be. The Nav should always lead to other pages from your website.

What goes in Main?

The main content/purpose of your page goes inside the main.

What goes in the Footer?

The final small notes like copyright go inside the footer.
