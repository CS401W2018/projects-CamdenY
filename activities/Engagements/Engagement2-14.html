Engagement - More CSS

What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

Block tags take up the width of the entire page by default. They have a height, width and. Inline tags do not have these and surround text instead.

What is the margin, border, and padding?

The spacing is the space between our element and the border, while our border is the amount of space outlining our element. The margin, is the space between our element and other elements.

What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

Pxl, cm, in, %, and em.

When would you use a figure tag?

You would use a figure tag when you want to use an image without a line break.
