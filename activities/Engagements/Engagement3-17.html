The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?

The action attribute is where the information of the form is being sent to.

The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?

The method attribute dictates if the form changes the data on the server.

What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?

Post updates a servers data while get retrieves it.

What are different ways to style your form?

You can use text align to line up the text in your forms. You can also create classes to apply to forms to space them out equally and align them.

Why does the professor like to use flex?

Flex lets the site automatically wrap items according to device size.
