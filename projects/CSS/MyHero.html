Hero - Steven Spielberg

Interest Hero

Steven Spielberg is a famous American director. He is one of the most influential and most successful directors of all time, as he is seen as the father of the modern blockbuster and his movies have made billions of dollars at the box office. He is well known for his ability to capture emotions through the framing and movement of the camera. Combined with excellent musical scores and iconic lead performances, Steven Spielberg's best movies are unforgettable experiences that will stick with you for years after you've finished viewing.

Jurassic Park
Jurassic Park was released in 1993 and is Spielberg's most succesful movie at the box office, making $1.058 billion. The film stars Sam Neill, Laura Dern and Jeff Goldblum as they are trapped on a theme park island with living dinosaurs. The movie is well known for not just it's suspenseful scenes of dinosaurs pursuing our heroes, but also for it's sense of wonder that the movie portrays. It features an iconic score by John Williams, a longtime contributor for Spielberg's movies.
Raiders of the Lost Ark
Raiders of the Lost Ark was released in 1981 and is the first movie in the Indiana Jones series. It features daring archaelogist Indiana Jones as he races against the Nazis to find the Lost Ark. The Indiana Jones series is Spielberg's foray back into the campy adventure serials of the 30's and 40's. It's also Spielberg's longest running franchise, with the newest installment releasing in 2023. To read some more about the third installment Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade, click the Interest link above.
Jaws
Jaws was released in 1975 and is another one of Spielberg's most famous films. Jaws is about the tourist town Amity Island dealing with a bloodthirsty shark. This is one of Spielbergs only horror movies, and it is iconic in how it kept it's audience dreading the arrival of the shark. Jaws also features one of the most iconic horror stingers ever in it's soundtrack.
